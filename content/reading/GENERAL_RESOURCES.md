## 📝 B2 EXAM READING QUESTION TYPES

### Type A: Multiple Choice (SINGLE answer)
> Question: "Was ist die Hauptaussage des Textes?"
> 4 options, only 1 correct. Tests global understanding.

### Type B: True/False (Richtig/Falsch)
> Statement given, decide if it matches the text.
> Often has a justification question ("Warum?")

### Type C: Fill-in-the-Blank (Lückentext)
> Sentence with ___ → fill with correct word/phrase
> Tests vocabulary and grammar in context

### Type D: Matching (Zuordnungsaufgabe)
> Connect statements to paragraphs (in longer texts)
> Tests local detail understanding

### Type E: Word Selection (Wortwahl)
> "Welches Wort passt in die Lücke?"
> Often grammar-based (preposition, article, verb form)

## 🎯 READING STRATEGIES FOR B2 EXAM

### Before Reading
1. **Skim questions first** – know what to look for
2. **Note question words** – Was? Wer? Wo? Wann? Warum?
3. **Estimate text type** – news article, opinion, instruction?

### During Reading
1. **First read**: Get the gist (整体印象)
2. **Second read**: Answer specific questions
3. **Mark key sections** with eyes
4. **Ignore unknown words** – guess from context

### Time Management
| Text Type | Time | Strategy |
|-----------|------|----------|
| Short (150-200 words) | 3-4 min | Read once, answer |
| Medium (250-350 words) | 5-6 min | Read twice, answer |
| Long (400+ words) | 7-8 min | Skim → questions → detail read |

## ⚠️ COMMON B2 EXAM PITFALLS

1. **Numbers trap** – Statement says "80%" but text says "18%". READ CAREFULLY!
2. **Negation trap** – "nicht", "kein", "nie" change everything in True/False
3. **"Im Text steht..."** – Only what EXPLICITLY is in the text, not your opinion
4. **Time pressure** – Don't spend 10 min on one text. Move on and return.
5. **Fill-in-blank guessing** – Leave it blank = 0 points. Guess if unsure!
